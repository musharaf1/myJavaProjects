ABOUT TN SERVICES

TN SERVICES is a privately owned real estate development company operating out of Lagos, Nigeria. Our focus is to bring clarity to, and demystify the process of buying and selling real estate in Nigeria; especially in Lagos State. All our properties are free from government acquisition, omonile trouble or any other encumbrance whatsoever. We offer other value added services including but not limited to legal advice on property matters, architectural drawings etc. Under the leadership of our amiable chairman, TN, our mission is to render top-notch services to our numerous satisfied clients while maintaining the highest standards of integrity. With a network of over 300 associate marketing partners, our reach covers the real estate industry across Nigeria, and all over the world.
