ABOUT SCHOLARPH INSTITUTE

Our Vision

To be a leading institution offering Islamic and conventional trainings, and by which disciplined, empowered and resourceful individuals who would be globally competent are raised in moral and standard.

Our Mission

Raising competent, adaptive, resourceful and empowered individuals, under divine guidance, that are productive and contributive to development across all levels
